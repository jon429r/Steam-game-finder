What Is Steam Game Finder?

Steam game finder is our submittion for the database final project. This project solves the problem of finding games that you will enjoy playing. Most people have a hard time finding games that they will enjoy playing and they spend lots of time scrolling through steam to find that perfect game. Steam game finder solves this problem by allowing you to search for games based on the criteria that you want. This allows you to find games that you will enjoy playing in a much shorter amount of time.

How to use

Its up to you, You can use the search bar to look for specific games and add them to liked or disliked lists with the like and dislike buttons.
